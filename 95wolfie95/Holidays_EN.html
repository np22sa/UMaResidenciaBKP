Epiphany : One of three major Christian celebrations along with Christmas and Easter.

Fasching : Processions where people generate lots of noise and use horrifying face masks. Also a festival to honour the Goddess Freya.

Feast of the Assumption of Mary : It is the principal feast of the Blessed Virgin, the mother of Jesus Christ.

Oktoberfest : The world's largest beer festival. It takes place in Munich, Bavaria.

Corpus Christi : A catholic festival celebrated on the second thursday after 'Whitsun'.

German Unification Day : Celebrates the reunification of Germany, which took place on 3rd October 1990.

Reformation Day : Commemorates the day in 1517 when a German monk named Martin Luther (1483-1546) strode up to the church in Wittenburg and nailed his 95 'theses' (or propositions) to the church's door.

All Saints' Day : Commemoration day for all Christian saints.
