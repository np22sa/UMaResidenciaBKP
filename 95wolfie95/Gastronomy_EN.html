Rouladen : Thinly sliced meat wrapped around a filling of bacon, chopped onions and pickles.

Eintopf : Made from a broth, vegetables, potatoes and then some meat or fish.

Sauerbraten : Can be made from many different meats, which are marinated in wine, vinegar, spices and herbs.

Brezel : Soft, white pretzels made from flour water and yeast and sprinkled with salt (and sometimes different seeds).
